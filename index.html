Welcome to my homepage!

My name is Siavash Rashidi-Sabet. I am a doctoral candidate in Marketing and Supply Chain Management at the Rawls College of Business. Prior to joining Texas Tech University, I earned an M.Sc. in Financial Economics from the University of Sheffield and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the Iran University of Science and Technology. My research interests include marketing strategy, social dilemmas, corporate social responsibility, sustainability, and capability building. My work has received both internal and external grants/support/recognition and appeared/accepted in the Journal of Business Logistics, Journal of Business Research, and Journal of Macromarketing.
