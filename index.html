Welcome to my homepage!

My name is Siavash Rashidi-Sabet. I am a doctoral candidate in Marketing at the Rawls College of Business. Prior to joining Texas Tech University, I earned an M.Sc. in Financial Economics from the University of Sheffield and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the Iran University of Science and Technology. My research interests include marketing strategy, marketing theory, social dilemmas, corporate social responsibility, sustainability, and capability building. My work has appeared in the Journal of Business Research and Journal of Macromarketing. I won the 2021 AMS Review/Sheth Foundation Doctoral Competition for Conceptual Articles (ADCCA) Award, and I have presented my work at the AMA Winter Academic Conference and the Academy of Marketing Science Annual Conference. Before starting my doctoral program, I worked as an analyst in the marketing and financial domains. I built marketing mix models to optimize marketing activities and developed automated systems to analyze, measure, and predict marketing outcomes.
